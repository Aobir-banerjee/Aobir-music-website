Track Details

Purono Chithi

Description: This album contains 9 tracks, exploring various moods and themes. It blends elements of Bangladeshi and Indian culture.

Songs in the Album

1. Lal Golapi

A soulful track celebrating love with rich, heartfelt lyrics and melody.

2. Ei Meghla Dine Ekla

A nostalgic song reflecting on solitude and melancholy on a cloudy day.

3. Anondoloke

A beautiful composition by Tagore, conveying joy and celebration through poetic lyrics.

4. Bhalo Achi Bhalo Theko

A melancholic farewell song expressing love and well-wishing.

5. Oi Dur Durante

A poignant song about distance and longing for someone far away.

6. Prochondo Gorjone

A powerful, dramatic composition that portrays inner turmoil and emotional intensity.

7. Shokhi Bhabona Kahare Bole

A reflective song about unspoken emotions and the complexities of love.

8. Deho Ghori

A traditional folk song celebrating the rhythms of life and nature.

9. Dhonodhanney Pushpo Bhora

A patriotic and spiritual song that emphasizes the beauty of the land and its prosperity.
